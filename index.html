Their Story

Mumford and Sons are an English-Irish folk band from London, UK. They formed in 2007 and have gained worldwide popularity since then. They have released four studio albums: Sigh No More (2009), Babel (2012), Wilder Mind (2015), and Delta (2018). They have also won numerous awards including a Grammy in 2011 for Album of the Year.
